# Carlos Iguaran
 
Computer scientist (MSc, UBA - University of Buenos Aires, 2017).  
Specialist in Mathematical Statistics (UBA, 2025).
 
I am interested in the computational aspects of **Bayesian inference** — in particular, modelling statistical problems as programs and implementing inference algorithms such as MCMC and SMC.
 
> I strongly believe that mastering both theory and craft requires time. Over the past decade, I've pursued roles that allowed me to develop complementary skills.
